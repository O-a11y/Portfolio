App Inventor Project: Space Invaders

From September to October 2019, Pranav Guda and I created a mini-game based off the shooting game Space Invaders. Our version of the game has an Area 51 theme, and you must stop "Naruto runners" from reaching Area 51.


In this game, your character is a fictional Area 51 guard. Naruto runners, with two different costumes, will come down and try to reach Area 51. Your job, as the player, is to prevent this from happening by shooting the Naruto runners as they appear, in a fashion similar to the classic game Space Invaders. You earn points for each kill, and as you do, the Naruto runners will change direction to provide an extra challenge. The aim is to garner as many points as you can.

Note: This mini-game is currently not available to the public for playing. Sorry for the inconvenience.
